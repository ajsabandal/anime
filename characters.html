Characters of the Week!

Rating: 95%

Rintaro Okabe

"I'm a Mad Scientist!"

Okabe has the characteristics of a lanky young man, with a square jaw and bony cheeks. He has his short black hair swept back and has brown irises, with a small amount of stubble on his chin. Rintaro also wears a lab coat, which he wears over a simple shirt and slacks. In the game, Okabe appeared much "dustier". His face was pale and unshaven and he seemed a lot more "messy" in contrast to his relatively "clean" appearance in the anime.

Rating: 92%

Izuki Midoriya

"All men are not equal"

Though born without a Quirk, he manages to catch the attention of the legendary hero All Might due to his innate heroism and has since become his close pupil and also a student at U.A. High School. All Might passed on his quirk to Izuku, making Izuku the ninth owner of the Quirk One For All.

Rating: 90%

Eren Jaeger

”It’s humanity’s turn now.”

Eren Yeager is a member of the Survey Corps and the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. He lived in the Shiganshina District with his parents and adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman until the fall of Wall Maria. During the incident, Eren impotently witnessed his mother being eaten by a Titan. This event aroused in Eren an intense hatred towards the Titans, and he swore to wipe all of them off the face of the Earth.

Rating: 88%

Shouto Todoroki

"Isn't it a hero's job to save people"

Shoto is a teenage boy who is relatively tall for his age. He has short hair, evenly split between white on his right-side and red on his left, visibly reflecting his Quirk. He has a burn scar over his left eye from childhood. He possesses heterochromia which causes his left eye to be turquoise and his right eye to be gray. In a few episodes, it's seen that he has an impressive physique, noted by Kohei Horikoshi himself in Shoto's Manga profile. Several girls have claimed that he is incredibly handsome, despite his scar. Mina even claimed that he is the best looking guy in the class.

Rating: 93%

Hanesaki Ayano

"Forget Mom"

Ayano Hanesaki (Japanese:羽咲 綾乃 Hanesaki Ayano) is one of the main protagonists of Hanebado! written by Kōsuke Hamada. She is a first year at Kitakomachi High and is a member of the badminton club. In the anime she never intended to join the club, but with some unexpected circumstances she was forced to join. In the manga, she found a reason on why she should join the club.

Rating: 91%

Aqua

"Baka!!"

Aqua (アクア) is one of the main protagonists of the KonoSuba series. Prior to life in the Fantasy World, she was a Goddess of Water who guided humans in the afterlife. She is worshiped in the Fantasy World by the Axis Order.

Rating: 90%

Megumin

"Explosion!"

Megumin is an Arch Wizard of the Crimson Magic Clan in the Fantasy World, and is the first person to join Kazuma's party. She is one of the main characters of the KonoSuba series.

Rating: 88%

Nagisa

"What is my purpose?"

Nagisa Aragaki(Japanese:荒垣 なぎさ Aragaki Nagisa) is a third year at Kitakomachi High and the captain of Kitakomachi's badminton club. Getting skunked by a middle school student at the Inter-High has caused her to go in a slump, hindering her talent. This was later fixed by Kentarou Tachibana.
